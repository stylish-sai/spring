## üí° What is FinTech?

**FinTech (Financial Technology)** refers to the use of technology to improve, automate, and innovate financial services such as payments, lending, investments, insurance, and compliance.

> FinTech focuses on making financial services **faster, cheaper, more accessible, and user-friendly** compared to traditional financial institutions.

---

## üîë Key Characteristics of FinTech

- **Technology-driven**: Cloud, APIs, AI/ML, mobile-first systems  
- **Customer-centric**: Focus on UX and accessibility  
- **Agile & innovative**: Faster development cycles  
- **Data-intensive**: Heavy use of analytics and automation  
- **Disruptive**: Challenges legacy banking models

## üåç Real-World Case Study: UPI (India)

**What**: Real-time payment system  
**Launched**: 2016  

**Impact**
- 100+ billion transactions annually
- Interoperable across banks
- Zero-cost transactions
- Simple QR / mobile-based UX

**Why it succeeded**
- Government support
- Strong regulatory framework
- Excellent user experience
